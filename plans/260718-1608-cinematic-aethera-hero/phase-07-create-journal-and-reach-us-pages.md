# Phase 7: Create Journal and Reach Us pages

## Overview

Complete the editorial Journal index and an honest, accessible contact experience.

## Implementation Steps

1. Add typed journal entries with topic, reading time, date, excerpt, and stable anchors.
2. Build Journal with a lead story, article grid, and newsletter-style contact CTA.
3. Build Reach Us with contact channels, engagement expectations, and accessible validation.

- [ ] Both pages pass keyboard and responsive checks.
